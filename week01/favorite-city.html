My Favourite City

My Favourite City, Harare. It is the capital of Zimbabwe. It is a city well known for its vibrancy and a modern urban center located in the northeastern part of the country. Established in 1890 and originally named Salisbury, the city was renamed in 1982 after independence. It serves as the nation's political, commercial and cultural hub. This encompasses tree-lined avenues, bustling markets with a mixture of colonial and contemporary achitecture.
