Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe is a landlocked country, located in the Southern Hemisphere of Africa. It is surrounded by countries such as South Africa, Botswana, Mozambique and Zambia. It is a diverse country with three main languages which are English, Shona and Ndebele. It is a country with a diverse number of ethnicities, many of their roots embedded with the nguni tribes located around the southern part of the continent. It is a country with one of the natural wonders of the world, majestic wildlife and beautiful sunsets.
